There was many historical events inthat took place worldwide during the 1910s. This page will list some of the interesting or important events that took place each year.


World Timeline (1910-1919)


1910

  • King Edward VII dies
  • The Houndsditch murders take place

1911

  • Machu Picchu is discovered
  • The first use of aircrafts as an offensive weapon takes place in the Turkish-Italian War
  • The first International Women's Day is observed by Denmark, Austria, Switzerland, and Germany

1912

  • Hsian-T'ung, the last emperor of China, is forced to abdicate.
  • Taiwan is founded

1913

  • The first crossword puzzle is published
  • The Rite of Spring by Stravinsky debuts

1914

  • Archduke Francis Ferdinand is assassinated
  • World War I begins
  • The Empress of Ireland sinks
  • Great Britain places Egypt under protection of the Crown
  • The Panama Canal opens
  • The WWI Christmas Truce takes place

1915

  • Zeppelin raids begin in England
  • Lethal chemical weapons are used in the war
  • The Formidable, a British warship, is sunk by a German submarine
  • The British and French Allied forces launch a naval attack against Turkish forces in the Dardanelles

1916

  • Pancho Villa attacks Columbus, New Mexico
  • Rasputin is murdered
  • The Battle of Jutland takes place
  • The Battle of the Somme takes place
  • The Easter uprising takes place in Ireland
  • The Battle of Verdun takes place

1917

  • The Russian Revolution takes place
  • The Battle of Jerusalem takes place
  • Constitution of Mexico is ratified
  • The Battle of Langemarck takes place
  • The Third Battle of Ypres takes place
  • The Second Battle of Ramadi takes place
  • The Second Russian Revolution takes place

1918

  • The "Red Baron" is killed in action
  • The Second Battle of Marne takes place
  • The Hindenburg Line is broken by Allied forces
  • World War I ends
  • The Armistice of Mudros is signed
  • The United Kingdom grants limited voting rights to women
  • Iceland becomes independent

1919

  • Benito Mussolini establishes the Fascist Party
  • Treaty of Versailles is signed
  • The League of Nations is created
  • Paris Peace Conference takes place
