The 1910s


Home - U.S. Events - Foreign Events - Fashion - Entertainment - Inventions - Food



The 1910s were filled with many amazing inventions, some of which would impact every decade to come.

Some inventions made during this decade include:


1910

  • Headsets
  • Neon lamps
  • Kitchen mixers

1911

  • Binder clips
  • Autombile self starters
  • Stenotype machines
  • Rutherford atom model

1912

  • Autopilot
  • Electric blankets
  • Electric traffic lights
  • Oreo cookies
  • Life Savers candies

1913

  • Formica (a type of plastic)
  • Zippers

1914

  • Traffic cones
  • Fortune cookies
  • Regenerative circuits

1915

  • Skeet shooting

1916

  • Hamburger buns
  • Lincoln Logs
  • Supermarkets
  • Tow trucks
  • Toggle light switches
  • Sonar

1917

  • Marshmallow creme
  • Clark bars

1918

  • Torque wrenches
  • Grocery bags
  • Hydraulic brakes

1919

  • Blenders
  • Silica gel
  • Pop-up toasters
